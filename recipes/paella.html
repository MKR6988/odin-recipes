Paella

Ingredients

  • 1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 pound mussels, scrubbed and debearded
  • 1 pound clams, scrubbed
  • 4 chicken thighs, bone-in and skin-on
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup canned diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup short-grain rice
  • 1/2 teaspoon saffron threads
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lemon wedges for serving

Preparation

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large paella pan or skillet over medium-high heat. Season the chicken thighs with salt and pepper and add them to the pan, skin-side down. Cook until the skin is browned and crispy, then flip and cook for another 3-4 minutes. Remove from the pan and set aside.
  2. Add the onion, garlic, and bell peppers to the pan and saut√© for 3-4 minutes, until the vegetables are softened.
  3. Add the diced tomatoes, smoked paprika, and saffron to the pan and stir to combine.
  4. Add the rice to the pan and stir to coat it in the tomato mixture. Cook for 1-2 minutes, stirring frequently.
  5. Add the chicken broth to the pan and bring to a simmer. Return the chicken thighs to the pan, along with any accumulated juices. Cover the pan and simmer for 20-25 minutes, until the rice is cooked and the liquid is absorbed.
  6. Meanwhile, steam the mussels and clams in a separate pot until they open. Discard any that do not open.
  7. Add the shrimp, mussels, and clams to the paella pan and cook for another 5-10 minutes, until the shrimp are pink and cooked through.
  8. Garnish with lemon wedges and serve immediately.
