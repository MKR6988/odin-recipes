Hamburger

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef (80% lean, 20% fat)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 4 slices American cheese
  • 4 hamburger buns, split and toasted
  • 4 lettuce leaves
  • 4 tomato slices
  • 4 slices red onion
  • Ketchup and mustard for serving

Preparation

  1. Preheat a grill or grill pan to medium-high heat.
  2. Divide the ground beef into 4 equal portions and shape into patties.
  3. Sprinkle the patties with salt and pepper.
  4. Grill the patties for 3-4 minutes per side, or until cooked to your desired level of doneness.
  5. During the last minute of cooking, add a slice of cheese to each patty.
  6. Assemble the burgers by placing a lettuce leaf, tomato slice, and onion slice on the bottom half of each bun. Add a cooked patty with cheese on top. Top with ketchup and mustard as desired.
  7. Place the top half of each bun on the burger and serve immediately.
