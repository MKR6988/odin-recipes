Pizza

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Pizza sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings of your choice (e.g. pepperoni, mushrooms, onions, peppers)

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F (218°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to the desired size and thickness.
  3. Spread the pizza sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  4. Add the mozzarella cheese and your desired toppings.
  5. Bake the pizza for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
