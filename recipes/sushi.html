Sushi

Ingredients

  • Sushi rice
  • Sushi vinegar
  • Nori (dried seaweed sheets)
  • Sushi fillings of your choice (e.g. avocado, cucumber, salmon, tuna)
  • Soy sauce
  • Wasabi paste
  • Pickled ginger

Preparation

  1. Rinse the sushi rice in cold water until the water runs clear. Cook the rice according to the package instructions.
  2. Once the rice is cooked, transfer it to a large bowl and add the sushi vinegar. Mix well to combine.
  3. Place a sheet of nori on a sushi mat or a clean kitchen towel.
  4. Spread a thin layer of rice over the nori, leaving a 1-inch border at the top edge.
  5. Add your desired fillings in a horizontal line across the center of the rice.
  6. Roll the sushi tightly away from you, using the mat or towel to shape it into a cylinder. Press down gently to seal the edges.
  7. Cut the sushi roll into bite-sized pieces with a sharp knife.
  8. Serve the sushi with soy sauce, wasabi paste, and pickled ginger.
