Biryani

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice, soaked for 30 minutes and drained
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch piece fresh ginger, peeled and grated
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 4 whole cloves
  • 4 green cardamom pods
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon coriander seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon black peppercorns
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 2 cups water
  • Salt to taste
  • Fried onions, chopped cilantro, and sliced green chilies for serving

Preparation

  1. In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the onions and cook until golden brown, stirring occasionally, about 10 minutes.
  2. Add the garlic and ginger and cook for another minute.
  3. Add the chicken and cook until browned on all sides, about 5-7 minutes.
  4. Reduce the heat to low and add the yogurt, bay leaves, cloves, cardamom pods, cinnamon stick, cumin seeds, coriander seeds, black peppercorns, turmeric, and red chili powder. Stir to combine.
  5. Add the rice and water and bring to a boil. Season with salt to taste.
  6. Reduce the heat to low, cover, and simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the rice is cooked and the liquid has been absorbed.
  7. Remove from heat and let rest for 5-10 minutes.
  8. Fluff the rice with a fork and transfer to a serving platter. Top with fried onions, chopped cilantro, and sliced green chilies. Serve hot.
