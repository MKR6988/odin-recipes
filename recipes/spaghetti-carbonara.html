Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 1/2 pound pancetta or bacon, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 1 cup grated Pecorino Romano cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • Salt to taste

Preparation

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the spaghetti and cook according to package instructions until al dente.
  2. Meanwhile, heat a large skillet over medium heat. Add the pancetta or bacon and cook until crispy, stirring occasionally, about 8-10 minutes.
  3. Add the garlic to the skillet and cook for an additional minute, or until fragrant.
  4. In a medium bowl, whisk together the egg yolks, Pecorino Romano cheese, and black pepper.
  5. When the spaghetti is cooked, reserve 1 cup of the pasta cooking water, then drain the spaghetti.
  6. Immediately add the spaghetti to the skillet with the pancetta or bacon and garlic. Toss to combine and coat the spaghetti with the rendered fat.
  7. Remove the skillet from heat, then quickly stir in the egg yolk mixture until it coats the spaghetti evenly. Add pasta water a tablespoon at a time, as needed, to create a creamy sauce.
  8. Season with salt to taste and serve immediately.
