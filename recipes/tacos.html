Tacos

Ingredients

  • Taco shells or tortillas
  • Ground beef or chicken
  • Taco seasoning
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Shredded cheese
  • Sour cream
  • Salsa
  • Guacamole

Preparation

  1. Cook the ground beef or chicken in a skillet over medium heat until browned and cooked through.
  2. Add the taco seasoning and stir to combine.
  3. Warm the taco shells or tortillas in the oven or microwave.
  4. Fill the shells or tortillas with the cooked meat, lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, sour cream, salsa, and guacamole, as desired.
  5. Serve immediately.
