Pad Thai

Ingredients

  • Rice noodles
  • Shrimp or chicken (or tofu for a vegetarian option)
  • Garlic
  • Bean sprouts
  • Green onions
  • Chopped peanuts
  • Eggs
  • Soy sauce
  • Fish sauce
  • Sugar
  • Lime wedges
  • Cilantro

Preparation

  1. Soak the rice noodles in cold water for 30 minutes.
  2. Cook the shrimp or chicken (or tofu) in a skillet over medium heat until cooked through. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
  3. Add the garlic to the skillet and sauté for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Add the bean sprouts and green onions to the skillet and sauté for another 1-2 minutes.
  5. Push the vegetables to the side of the skillet and crack the eggs into the empty space. Scramble the eggs until cooked through.
  6. Add the drained noodles to the skillet along with the soy sauce, fish sauce, and sugar. Toss to combine.
  7. Add the cooked shrimp or chicken (or tofu) back to the skillet and toss to combine.
  8. Top the pad thai with chopped peanuts, cilantro, and a squeeze of lime juice.
  9. Serve immediately.
